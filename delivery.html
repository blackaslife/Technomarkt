Delivery

We deliver goods to all regions of Poland. Delivery is carried out by transport companies, as well as by our own transport, depending on the size and weight of the order.

Delivery of goods is carried out within 1-3 working days from the moment of order confirmation. In case of unavailability of the goods in stock, the delivery time may be extended to 7 working days. The exact delivery time will be specified by the manager when confirming the order.

The cost of delivery depends on the region and the weight of the order. The exact cost will be specified by the manager when confirming the order.

When receiving the goods, please check the integrity of the packaging and the correctness of the items in the order. In case of damage or discrepancy, you have the right to refuse to accept the goods and draw up an act of discrepancy or damage.

For more information about delivery, please contact our manager by phone +44 7911 123456 or email:customerservice@technomart.com.uk
