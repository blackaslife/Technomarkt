More about our company

About Us

Welcome to Technomart, your one-stop shop for all building materials and repair tools. We are committed to providing high-quality products at competitive prices.

Our company was founded in 2010 and has since grown to become a leading supplier in the industry. We pride ourselves on our customer service and our knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist you with your needs.

Whether you are a professional contractor or a DIY enthusiast, we have everything you need to complete your projects successfully.

Visit us at our store in Poznań or browse our online catalog to discover our wide range of products.

Our History

Technomart was established in 2010 with the goal of providing high-quality building materials and tools to customers in Poznań and beyond. Over the years, we have expanded our product range and improved our services to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

We have built strong relationships with leading manufacturers, allowing us to offer a wide selection of products at competitive prices. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the industry.

As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to innovation and excellence, continuously seeking ways to enhance our offerings and better serve our customers.

Our Values

  • Quality: We believe in providing only the best products to our customers.
  • Customer Service: Our customers are our top priority, and we strive to exceed their expectations.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency.
  • Innovation: We are always looking for new ways to improve our products and services.

Our Team

At Technomart, we have a dedicated team of professionals who are passionate about what they do. Our staff includes experienced sales representatives, knowledgeable product specialists, and skilled technicians who are always ready to assist you.

We believe that our people are our greatest asset, and we invest in their training and development to ensure they are equipped with the latest industry knowledge and skills.

Meet our team members who are committed to helping you find the right solutions for your building and repair needs.

Our Mission

At Technomart, our mission is to provide high-quality building materials and tools that empower our customers to complete their projects with confidence. We aim to be the preferred choice for both professionals and DIY enthusiasts by offering a wide range of products, exceptional customer service, and competitive prices.

We are committed to sustainability and strive to minimize our environmental impact through responsible sourcing and efficient operations.

Our goal is to build lasting relationships with our customers and contribute positively to the communities we serve.
