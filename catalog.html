A PAIR OF WORDS ABOUT ROTARY HUMMER

Rotary hammer — universal construction tool. Designed for drilling and chiselling holes in brick, concrete and stone.
There are electromechanical and pneumatic rotary hammers. Modern models can perform the functions of a drill, screwdriver and jackhammer. There is a choice of "three-mode" in the product range: a jackhammer and an impact drill will be combined in one device.
